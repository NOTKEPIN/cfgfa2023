TESTIMONIALS

Since 2021, SNM has received over $500,000 in goods that have been distributed throughout the Bay Area.

Alham B., his wife, and 2 children have only been in the U.S. for 2 months. Alham B. stated:

“This is such a blessing. We were forced to leave our home in Eastern Europe and settle here in Antioch with very few belongings. I came to the event hoping to get some help for my children and my family is leaving with new shoes and clothes. Thank you, we are truly grateful.”

Erica C., a single mother of 7 children:

“We came here to see what this event was about, and I was shocked to see what was offered and to see that you have items for everyone in my family. It was amazing. Buying shoes and clothes for my older kids is expensive and I can’t always get them new things at the same time. I was fortunate enough to find really nice new men and women sizes that they liked and that were in the sizes that I needed. Thank you for having this event.”

Jessica D. is a wife and mother of 4 children who cannot afford to pay for their youngest child's childcare. She is the sole breadwinner in her household and struggles to provide for her kids. SNM asked how receiving new shoes for everyone in her family would make a difference. She responded:

“Thank you, thank you, thank you. My youngest son who is 3 years old was wearing shoes that were too small and he is walking out of this event with a new pair of shoes and socks that are the right size. It's really overwhelming in here with how much you have to offer. Waking up every day praying for help and not knowing my help was in this building is truly a blessing. Thank you all for having this event.”

CURRENT PROGRAMS

Let's Do It Again
Let's Do It Again supports men ages 18 and up. Whether the men are re-entering society from incarceration, homelessness, or struggling with issues in everyday life. Services include job readiness/training and job referrals, etiquette and grooming classes, grooming care kits, free clothing and shoes for everyday wear and interview and work attire. We also offer parenting classes and peer group sessions to support our participants in voicing concerns, sharing their fears and insecurities, celebrating each new victory, and encouraging them to perservere when things do not go as planned.
Hi, Remember Me?
Hi, Remember Me? focuses on helping youth participants ages 11-18 who feel forgotten or stigmatized by negative protrayals. We help them understand how they show up in today's world and empowering them to become who they are meant to be inthe future. This is done through group sessions with like-minded youth who want to work to change the narrative. We bring guest speakers who share life stories and encourage the youths to be change agents for the future. We also teach etiquette classes, budgeting and basic financial literacy, grooming, and cooking classes.
Refresh 'N Renew Program
Refresh 'N Renew offers 6 Community Giveaways throughout the year culminating in our Holiday Giveaway in the month of December for the entire family. Available items include free clothing, shoes, home items including beds and bedding, decor, furniture. Based on availability, kitchenware, health and beauty products, accessories, and baby items.
